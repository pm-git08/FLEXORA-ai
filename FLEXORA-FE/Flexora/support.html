Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to account settings → security → reset password. You’ll receive an email with instructions.

How do I contact my client?

Use the Flexora inbox located in your dashboard. It supports file sharing, scheduling, and chat.

How do I get paid?

Payments are processed through Flexora’s secure gateway. Link your bank or wallet in Payment Settings.
